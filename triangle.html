Triangle

A triangle is a polygon with three sides. They can be all the same size (equililaderal), two the same, one different (isosceles), or all different (scalene).
Triangles can also be right (one right angle), acute (all angles less than 90 degrees), or obtuse (one angle greater than 90 degrees).
